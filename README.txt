== Description ==

Motors VIN Decoder & Vehicle History Check is free plugin to decode your vehicle VIN. Free version is based on USA National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) database. Decode a Vehicle Identification Number (VIN), obtain manufacturer data describing the vehicle basic information maintained by the USA government.

This version of the plugin uses NHTSA service to decode VIN numbers and provide vehicle information to a user. The service is free, and no extra setup needed. NHTSA — National Highway Traffic Safety Administration of US Department of Transportation provides FREE API Service for Car Specifications. Please note, NHTSA does not provide VIN History Data.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is VIN Number and how to use it? =

A VIN is composed of 17 characters (digits and capital letters) that act as a unique identifier for the vehicle. A VIN displays the car's unique features, specifications and manufacturer. The VIN can be used to track recalls, registrations, warranty claims, thefts and insurance coverage.

= How Motors VIN Decoder Plugin Works? =

When you enter a VIN number the plugin send request to a selected vehicle data provider, then shows the information on your website. You can decode VIN numbers, as well as check vehicle history records that is maintained by respective government authorities and/or third party services.
